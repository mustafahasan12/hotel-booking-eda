# 🏨 Hotel Booking Demand Analysis using Python

## 📌 Project Overview

This project performs an **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)** on the Hotel Booking Demand dataset to uncover booking patterns, customer behavior, cancellation trends, and pricing insights. The analysis includes data cleaning, visualization, and business recommendations that can help hotel management make data-driven decisions.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

This project aims to answer the following business questions:

- Which hotel type receives the highest number of bookings?
- What is the overall booking cancellation rate?
- Which customer segments generate the most bookings?
- How does Average Daily Rate (ADR) change throughout the year?
- Which market segments have the highest cancellation rates?
- What booking trends can help improve hotel revenue?

---

## 📂 Dataset

**Dataset Name:** Hotel Booking Demand Dataset

The dataset contains booking information for both **City Hotels** and **Resort Hotels**, including customer demographics, reservation details, pricing, cancellations, and stay information.

### Dataset Features

- Hotel Type
- Lead Time
- Arrival Date
- Country
- Market Segment
- Deposit Type
- Customer Type
- Average Daily Rate (ADR)
- Booking Status
- Cancellation Status
- Special Requests
- Stay Duration

# 📈 Key Business Insights

### 🏨 Hotel Bookings

- City Hotels receive significantly more bookings than Resort Hotels.
- Urban tourism and business travel appear to drive the majority of reservations.

---

### ❌ Booking Cancellations

- A considerable percentage of bookings are cancelled.
- Reducing cancellations could significantly improve occupancy and revenue.

---

### 💰 Average Daily Rate (ADR)

- ADR varies throughout the year, indicating strong seasonal pricing patterns.
- Premium room bookings contribute disproportionately to revenue.

---

### 🌍 Customer Analysis

- Most bookings originate from a limited number of countries.
- Marketing campaigns should focus on these high-performing markets.

---

### 📢 Market Segment

- Online Travel Agencies (OTAs) generate the largest share of bookings.
- Hotels should maintain strong OTA partnerships while encouraging direct bookings.

---

# 💼 Business Recommendations

- Increase staffing during peak booking months.
- Implement dynamic pricing strategies during high-demand seasons.
- Reduce booking cancellations through improved cancellation policies.
- Strengthen marketing campaigns in high-performing countries.
- Expand customer loyalty programs for frequent travelers.
- Promote direct bookings to reduce third-party commission costs.
